Social Media Browser Extension

Empowering parents with AI-driven content filtering to ensure a safe online experience

Why Does Your Child Need Social Media Protection?

In today’s digital world, children and teens are constantly exposed to social media content—some of which may be harmful, inappropriate, and offensive. While platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok provide entertainment and learning opportunities, they also harbor risks such as explicit videos, cyberbullying, and unsafe interactions. Safe Social's Social Media Browser Protection is designed to give parents peace of mind by automatically filtering harmful content before it reaches their child’s screen.

How It Works

Safe Social uses cutting-edge **AI technology** to analyze and block explicit images and harmful text content in **real-time**. Whether your child is watching YouTube, or scrolling through Instagram, the Safe Social browser extension ensures they only access safe content.

The system operates **seamlessly in the background**, requiring **no manual input** from parents once it's installed. You can customize filtering settings to suit different age groups, ensuring that content restrictions grow with your child.

Key Features

✔ AI-Based Filtering: Instantly detects and blocks harmful content, including nudity, violence, and explicit language.

✔ Safe YouTube Experience: Blocks inappropriate videos while allowing educational and age-appropriate content.

✔ Social Media Filtering: Restricts explicit images and dangerous trends across platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook.

Compatibility & Easy Setup

Safe Social works on Google Chrome and takes less than 2 minutes to install.

✅ Download the browser extension

✅ Disable social media app downloading on your child's phone

✅ Allow your child to access social media platforms like Instagram and YouTube through their Chrome browser

✅ Enjoy connecting with friends online, safe from harmful and malicious content!
