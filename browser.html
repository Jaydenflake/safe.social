Social Media Browser Extension

Empowering parents with AI-driven content filtering to ensure a safe online experience

Why Does Your Child Need Social Media Protection?

While platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok offer entertainment and educational opportunities, they also pose risks such as explicit videos, cyberbullying, and unsafe interactions. Safe Social's social media browser extension is designed to give parents peace of mind by automatically filtering harmful content before it reaches their child's screen.

How It Works

Safe Social uses cutting-edge AI technology to analyze and block explicit images and harmful text in real-time, ensuring your child only accesses safe content on platforms like YouTube and Instagram. The system operates seamlessly in the background, requiring no manual input once installed. You can also customize filtering settings to suit different age groups, adapting restrictions as your child grows.

Key Features

✔ AI-Based Filtering: Instantly detects and blocks harmful content, including nudity, violence, and explicit language.
